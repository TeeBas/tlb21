The Learning Bags

About Us

Who We Are


Owner and Founder

I am Thora Bastaldo, the owner and founder of The Learning Bags.
I always liked learning products and materials for younger students, whether I purchase them or make them myself.
I am very creative and like developing and making new items to aid others in the learning process from a tender age.

Our Story

How it all began

In the early months of the pandemic, I saw the need arising for learning materials for children to be used at home, as schools were closed, and a lot of persons did not have access to their own resources.
After seeing other products on the market, I decided to start up my own, unique collection of learning materials, that could be used at home and on the go.

Mission

Our plans

We aim to have every child being able to access and benefit from our custom designed products and materials.
Equiping parents, guardians and even teachers alike, with ideas to make the initial learning stages less stressful and more fun for everyone involved is also one of our main goals.

Vision


In the future, we see our products being manufactured on a much larger scale, being supplied to stores, and eventually the international market.
We see ourself being among one of the top suppliers of early learning products.

Thus, The Learning Bags was born.
